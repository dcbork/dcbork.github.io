Deep Sea Model


As a part of a Digital Modeling class at NC State I designed, printed and finished a model of semi-realistic deep sea organisms.

I model these animals and a base for them in Blender using sculpting and generative design.

After modeling them I printed them with a Elegoo Saturn resin printer with clear resin prints.

Finally I finsihed the prints and painted them with UV flourescent paint. I also built custom UV spotlights to light the model for photographing them.
